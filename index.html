ENGAGING GAMES

Puzzles Game

A question will appear and there would be 4 doors. 1 of the doors carries the correct solution which leads to the next round. The student can save their high score based on the number of rooms they enter.

Catch The Balls

A man standing on the ground level carrying a question, and solutions will fall from the sky in the form of balls. The man has to collect the right solution in a specific time based on the difficulty level of the question. Each game has 3 lives.

NOTES ACCESS/UPLOAD

Students or teachers can upload their notes for other students. As a person uploads notes, they will earn points, and reaching a certain amount of points will earn them reward money vouchers. Completing a particular course in a certain time rewards students with free study material.

Students can create a friend list, ask doubts, and share solutions with their peers.
